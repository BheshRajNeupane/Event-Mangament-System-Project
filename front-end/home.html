Our Services

We provide a comprehensive suite of services designed to meet your needs. Explore our offerings to discover how we can assist you:

  • User Registration: Easily sign up to become a member of our platform.
  • User Login: Securely sign in to access your events management service.
  • Event Creation: Create new events that are stored in the events.json file.
  • Event Viewing: Access and view detailed information about your events.
  • Event Filtering: Filter events by title, start date, or end date for easy navigation.
  • Event Updates: Modify and update your events as needed.
  • Event Deletion: Remove events that are no longer required.
